Welcome To Dungeon Seeking Dragon

We take the guess-work out of finding a D&D group so you can get playing!

Dungeons & Dragons is a tabletop roleplaying game, played with dice and friends! It's having something of a renaissance right now and more and more people are coming to the table. But sometimes you still just can't pull a group together and that's where we come in!

DsD is a "looking for group" app that helps Dungeons and Dragons players find groups to play with!
